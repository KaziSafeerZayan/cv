About Technology

We use technology a lot in our life. We use technology every day. Some technologies are computers, cars, TV, mobile phone, book, pen, notebook, headphones, bandage etc.



What Is Technology?

We are talking about technology. But what is it some of you might ask. Technology is the practical application of science that we use in our daily life. To simplify it, we can say that technology is the thing that is practically applied with science knowledge to create something that we use a lot in our life to make it easier to live.



Medical Technology

People get sick and they become ill. They become weak. They feel awful. That is why there is medical technology. An example of medical technology is the time of COVID-19 first and the most dangerous encountering. The scientists start making vaccines that are medical technology. These vaccines are the examples of medical technology. These now help us to survive. But there are also Ultrasonography, computerized tomography, X-ray machine, thermometer etc.

ICT

ICT is the type of technology that we use to know information and send it to the people of the world. ICT is the short for Information and Communication Technology. for example, computer, social media, TV etc.



Negative Effects

Even if there are a lot of Good things about technology, everything has its own ups and downs. The weapons are getting more dangerous with the advancements of technology. Vehicles make air pollution and horns make noise pollution. The best way to solve it is make more and using more electric cars.



Conclusion

So we can say that Technology has its ups and downs. The ups and downs depend on our use of it.
