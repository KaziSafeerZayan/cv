What The Universe Consists Of

The universe consists of all time and space that exists. Galaxies are the families of stars, planets and their satellites. The Milky Way Galaxy is the galaxy our solar system is in. The solar system is a family which consists of the Sun, planets and their satellites, comets, asteroids and so much more. The planets in our solar systems are 8 in number. They are Mercury, Venus, Earth Mars Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune and Jupiter Being the largest planet and having the mass of more than the other 7 planets combined. A satellite is an object that are revolves around a planet. There are also black holes in the Universe. There is a black hole in our galaxy named Sagittarius A. There are also nebulas. Nebulas are huge clouds of stars and gasses making beautiful figures. One of them in the Fox Fur Nebula. The universe is always expanding.
